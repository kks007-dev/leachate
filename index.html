Environmental Impact

Leachate - the liquid that drains or 'leaches' from a landfill - poses significant environmental challenges worldwide. It contains numerous harmful compounds that can contaminate groundwater and soil. Un-Leach technology addresses this critical issue through innovative filtering and treatment processes.


Our data shows that implementing proper leachate management systems can reduce contamination by up to 42%, preserving local ecosystems and protecting community health.

About Un-Leach

Un-Leach is a pioneering solution designed to address the growing environmental challenge of leachate management. Our technology combines advanced filtration systems with real-time monitoring to effectively process and treat leachate from landfills, industrial sites, and agricultural operations.


Founded in 2022, Un-Leach has quickly become a global leader in sustainable waste management solutions, with operations in over 45 countries.

Our Technology

The Un-Leach system utilizes a multi-stage filtration process that removes contaminants, heavy metals, and organic compounds from leachate. Each unit is equipped with smart sensors that continuously monitor water quality and system performance, ensuring optimal operation.


Our proprietary eco-friendly filtration media is designed to be regenerated rather than replaced, reducing waste and operational costs while maintaining superior performance.
